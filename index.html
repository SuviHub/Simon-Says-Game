How to Play Simon Says

The instructions to play Simon Says:

  • In this memory-based game, a sequence of colors will light up one by one.
  • Your goal is to repeat the sequence of colors in the exact same order as Simon shows you.
  • The sequence will start with one color, and each time you repeat the sequence correctly, it will get longer.
  • If Simon shows a color, you must click the corresponding button. If you fail to follow the correct order, you lose the game.
  • Each button will blink in the correct order, and you must remember and repeat that order.
  • Keep going until you make a mistake, and try to beat your high score!

Good luck and have fun!
